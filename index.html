Our tropical-themed gardens contain more than 40 different ponds, lakes and water features which produce the biggest display of lotus and waterlily flowers in the southern hemisphere. The grounds also contain an amazing array of exotic plants, garden exhibits, fountains, waterfalls, floral sculptures, walks and scenic vistas.

We have also provided extensive picnic facilities with numerous shelters and bbqs spread throughout the gardens. Alternatively, visitors can enjoy the wonderful hospitality of the Lily Pond Cafe that offers fantastic views of the flowers.

Autumn Flowers

See the amazing display of thousands of Autumn perennial and anuual flowers surrounding our lakes and ponds.

Giant Lilypads

See the World's biggest waterlily species that can hold heavy weights! Please note: Due to safety reasons, we do not allow visitors to sit or float on the lily pads.

River Walk & Bird Watching

Enjoy the wonders of our native enviroment with towering river gums and tree ferns. Along the river is a great place to catch a glimpse of many native birds.
